Business & Marketing Planning

This presentation analyzes Sense, an energy monitoring company, through a comprehensive business and marketing framework, including PESTLE, Porter's Five Forces, SWOT, and VRIO analyses. It identifies key challenges such as device detection accuracy, installation complexity, and user engagement, and proposes strategic, data-driven solutions to improve product performance and market expansion. As a result, the project highlights how Sense can leverage AI, partnerships, and user-centric innovations to stregthen its competitive positions in the smart energy market.

Financial Report for United Health

This presentation provides a comprehensive financial analysis of UnitedHealth Group from 2021-2024, highlighting strong revenue growth, resilient operational performance, and strategic expansion through acquisitions despite profitability pressures in 2024 caused by cybersecurity incidents and rising costs. It concludes that while the company faces short-term financial and operational challenges, its robust business model, market dominance, and long-term investment potential remain strong.
